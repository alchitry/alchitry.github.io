VBSEL A VBSEL B MV VCCO
floating floating 3.3
low low 3.3
low high 3.3
high low 1.8
high high 2.5
VBSEL B VBSEL A 2.5

low = 0-0.9 volts
high = 1.1-3.3 volts

Legend

IO N/P Differential capable IO
